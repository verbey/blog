Update on how things are going

Verbey
2023-12-17
meta, personal, studying, uni

A little preamble

When I published my last post, Useless work, I couldn’t even imagine it would only go downhill from there.

Now, let’s talk about why I haven’t posted anything in more than a month.

Computer Logic Coursework

Seriously, when you hear you will have to write 100+ pages of a coursework by hand, you think it can’t get any better than this.

At the very least, the subject for which I had to do that turned out to work by the “carrot and stick” formula. For my immeasurable suffering and sleepless nights, I was offered a 95% score on the subject and a 90% score on the coursework itself.

If you hand in the coursework before the deadline, which is not that hard, to be honest, then you are able to have the exam held online. Totally worth the hassle.

Oh, and I also discovered how much hatred I can have for 159 A4 pages of paper — a lot.

Computer Electronics

Do you know what is not worth the hassle, though? Computer electronics assignments!

The assignments are dead simple: replicate a scheme in Multisim, make some screenshots. Then calculate some formulas and compare the obtained data to that on the screenshots. It’s quite simple, despite taking a lot of time.

Too bad, the old fart checking the assignment doesn’t want your life to be simple.

To actually get a grade for the assignment, you must go through a circle of hell called “Захист роботи” (literally translated as “Defense of the assignment”, I can’t think of a proper alternative in English at the moment). You must answer a bunch of question from the professor, which are slightly SIGNIFICANTLY harder to answer than whatever you were preparing for.

Oh, so you were doing what for this work? What? I can’t hear you, sorry. Anyway, here’s a scheme I just made up in my head, calculate the voltage at its exit. Ah, wrong answer, 0/5 mark is what you deserve.

The whole group has low grades now, because we weren’t lucky enough to get a teacher who wouldn’t sabotage us with his cool questions aimed at computer geniuses.

By the way, we had to write the theory for the assignments by hand. Digitalisation didn’t go well in this place, as I can see.

Other subjects

Less interesting, but those still gave me enough pain in the ass.

Mostly because of how nice were the deadlines. Our assignments would be checked on week X, yet the lector thought it would be a good idea to put a deadline for uploading them to Moodle on week X-1.

These students must have such a lot of time, gotta make them work hard for once in their life.
– Every professor ever, probably.

What’s planned next for the blog

In between the suffering I’ve had different things happen and read some articles and played some games and coded some scripts etc. and all of that is blog-post-idea-provoking. Some posts are almost finished, for some I had only written down the titles.

Now that the semester has ended, I can dedicate time to the blog, which will make me and, hopefully, you happy.

I was also planning to get this place a nice domain, so you wouldn’t have to see the ugly GH Pages URL each time you go here.

May there be no misery in your life, reader.
Signing off,
Verbey
